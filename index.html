Places You Must Visit In Your Lifetime

Diseny Land Florida

I have visited on December 2018.

Epcot is a good theme park to visit especially for little kids and families. It's more laid back and has more kids ride than adult ones. You have different sections. The rides in land, Earth, sea .. etc section. Then you have the different countries represented in the park. Every country has local food to that county and a store. A introduction video or/plus a show. It took us about 6 hours to go to almost everything in a slow pace. We had a great time discovering everything. It was my first time but not the last.



Restaurant - Mehfil

Such, a variety of food, Chinese, Mexican, American, Punjabi, Gujarati, Japanese, Italian ect.

An marvellous place with affordable menu containing variety of punjabi food and starters, please note that in any festival please be there before time it took bit longer time in queue. In high demand because of good food and service!!
